LockedIn Figma Design

LockedIn is a productivity and focus app designed to help students, especially university students with ADHD, stay motivated to stay on top of their daily tasks and assignments. The app includes many features including a planner, a pet style companion, motivational quotes and other focus tools. The app tackles common struggles like procrastination, forgetfulness, and lack of motivation by providing a fun and supportive digital environment.
